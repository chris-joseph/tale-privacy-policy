Tale – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how Tale ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to transparency and ensuring your travel data remains secure.

1. Information We Collect

Currently, Tale operates as an offline-first application. Most data you input (itineraries, packing lists, and schedules) is stored locally on your device. However, to support upcoming features and improve your experience, we may collect the following in the future:

A. Personal Information

  • Account Details: If you create an account, we may collect your username, email address, and contact information.
  • Social Connections: For the upcoming "Travel Buddy" feature, we may collect data regarding your connections and friends within the app to facilitate shared planning.

B. Location Data

  • Precise Location: We may request access to your device's current and accurate GPS location. This is intended to help you find nearby attractions, navigate your trip, and provide location-specific travel tips.

C. Media and Gallery Access

  • Photo Library: Tale requires access to your Gallery/Photos. This allows you to personalise your trips by uploading photos to your itineraries, adding profile pictures, or attaching visual memories to your travel calendar.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the travel planning service.
  • Enable social features, such as connecting with friends or travel buddies.
  • Offer location-based services and navigation assistance.
  • Improve app performance and troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Currently, your data is primarily stored on your device. We recommend regular device backups to prevent data loss.
  • Cloud Integration: As we transition to online features (like syncing and social sharing), your data will be encrypted and stored on secure servers.
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security protocols to protect your personal information from unauthorised access or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Permissions

Tale may utilise third-party services (such as Google Play Services) that collect information used to identify you. When using location services, map providers may process your location data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. User Controls and Rights

You have the right to:

  • Grant or Revoke Permissions: You can manage access to your Location and Gallery at any time through your device settings.
  • Data Deletion: If account creation is implemented, you will have the option to delete your account and associated data.
  • Update Information: You can correct or update your personal details directly within the app settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect new features (like the social buddy system). We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
